About Me

I design and test mechanical, optical, and electrical systems for spatial computing products. I am part of the vision optics assembly team that develops and implements the mechanical design for the entire optical stack of our products. Responsible for maturing design from inception to production. I design test beds and fixtures for proof of concept builds. I work with 6-axis robots small and large, hexapods, and CMM machines
